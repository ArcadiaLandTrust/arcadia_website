Meet Arcadia

Arcadia is a non-profit land trust devoted to knitting together Middle Tennessee through creative land sharing, farming, and care networks.

Cooperation facilitation!

We foster cooperative land use by facilitating land sharing agreements.

Project Design & Planning

We develop and coordinate community-focused, community-led land design, emphasizing cutting-edge agroecology projects.

Community Partners

We strengthen Middle Tennessee's economy by linking local community organizations, businesses, and non-profits with projects to help land and people serve each other.

Secure Land

First and foremost, we hold land in trust, guaranteeing it will be used only for community & ecological benefit forever.
