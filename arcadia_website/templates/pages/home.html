Meet Arcadia

Arcadia land trust promotes community health in Middle Tennessee. We foster projects focused primarily on creative land use, cutting-edge sustainable farming, and land stewardship. We focus on reducing the substantial barriers to these activities so people can have secure and stable access to land doing exciting projects that matter to all of us, for the long term.

Cooperation facilitation!

We foster cooperative land use through creative, secure land sharing agreements.

Project Design & Planning

We develop and coordinate community-focused, community-led land projects, emphasizing cutting-edge agroecology practices.

Community Partners

We strengthen Middle Tennessee by linking community organizations, local businesses, and non-profits with viable land projects to enhance community interconnectivity.

Secure Land

First and foremost, we hold land in trust, guaranteeing it will be used only for community & ecological benefit forever.
