Meet Arcadia

Arcadia promotes community health in Middle Tennessee. We support projects focused on creative land sharing, cutting-edge perennial farming, and ecological land stewardship.

We envision a future in which anyone can secure access to land doing exciting projects that matter to all of us, for the long term.

Fosters cooperation.

Arcadia enables cooperative land use with creative & secure land sharing agreements.

Designs & plans projects.

We design and coordinate community-focused, community-led land projects, emphasizing cutting-edge agroecology & agroforestry practices.

Connects partners.

Arcadia links community organizations, local businesses, and non-profits with viable land projects to grow community resilience.
